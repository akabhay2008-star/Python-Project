# Delhi Weather Data – Summary Report

## Overview
This report analyzes a simple Delhi weather dataset containing date, temperature, humidity, and rainfall. It highlights basic trends and small anomalies typical of Delhi winters.

## Temperature Trends
- Temperature ranges from ~13°C to 21°C.
- A slow warming pattern appears across mid-January.
- Slight drops occur after rainfall.

## Humidity Patterns
- Humidity decreases from ~80% to ~50%.
- Lower humidity is associated with warmer days.

## Rainfall
- Mostly dry conditions.
- Light rainfall on a few days.
- Jan 14 records the highest value (1.6 mm).

## Anomalies
- Humidity spikes on colder days.
- Minor temperature dips after rainfall.

## Conclusion
Delhi’s winter shows: **rising temperatures**, **falling humidity**, and **minimal rainfall**, with short fluctuations linked to small rain events.
